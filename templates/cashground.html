Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cash-Ground?

Cash-Ground is a brand-new app that lets you earn real money by viewing ads in your free time! Who couldn’t use a few more bucks a day, especially when it’s absolutely spam-free and simple to use?

Get paid while:

  • Waiting for the microwave
  • Avoiding eye contact on public transportation
  • Dropping a slow one in the bathroom at work
  • Whenever you want! Wherever you want!

How does it work?

It’s simple! Launch Cash-Ground to browse our list of promotions and see what interests you. After completing a simple action such as watching a video or downloading an app, you are instantly credited with anywhere from 10¢ to over a dollar!

Why should I use it

Advertisers spend billions of dollars for the right to your eyeballs every year. Why shouldn’t you get a piece of it? In fact, they want you to use Cash-Ground, and that’s why they pay you! We verify that you actually watched an ad, and encourage you to find products you’re interested in and give them a try! This is how we create value for everyone, especially in the long run.

Where do I sign?

There are zero checks to sign—we deposit directly to your PayPal or bank! But if you mean you want to try our app, the best thing to do now is to pre-register your username, so that when we do launch very soon your favorite username-ever-since-middle-school doesn’t get taken.
